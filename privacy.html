Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Cute Count Down does not collect any data. We built it to be completely private.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or usage data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers or SDKs.

Where your data lives

The countdowns you create — names, dates, icons, and colors — are stored only on your device and are shared solely between the Cute Count Down app and its widgets on that same device. This information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page and revise the date above.
