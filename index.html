Frequently asked questions

How do I add a widget?

Long-press your Home Screen → tap + → search “Cute Countdown” → choose the Countdown, All Countdowns, or Add Countdown widget.

How do I choose which countdown a widget shows?

Long-press the Countdown widget → Edit Widget → pick the countdown you want.

How do I change how much detail a countdown shows?

When adding or editing a countdown, use the Detail option to pick days only, days & hours, or a full live timer.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything stays on your device — see our Privacy Policy.
